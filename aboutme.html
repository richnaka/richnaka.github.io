Who Am I?

I'm thrilled to have you here. My name is Richard Nakaoka, and I'm passionate about teaching. Currently, I teach Math I, Compressed Math, and Intro to Computer Science at Redlands East Valley High School. In the past, I've also taught 7th and 8th grade math, nurturing young minds with a love for numbers.



Originally from the beautiful beach cities of Los Angeles, I found my academic home at the University of Redlands. It's where I earned both my Bachelor's and Master's degrees. Redlands has become a cherished place for me, and I've happily made it my permanent residence.



When I'm not inspiring students in the classroom, I prioritize quality time with my family. I also have a deep appreciation for basketball especially NBA games. I enjoy exploring the world of video games, and relish cooking alongside my wife, who is a talented 4th-grade teacher at Judson & Brown Elementary.
